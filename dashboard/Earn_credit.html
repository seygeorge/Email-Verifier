    Users who sign up on our platform through your affiliate link are your direct referrals. You will receive a 15% commission on their instant credit purchases for the first 7 days, a 10% commission for up to 30 days, and a 5% commission for up to 180 days, with a maximum gift of 100,000 instant credits per transaction. The indirect affiliate attribution operates on a last-click basis, meaning if a user clicks on another affiliate's link after yours, they will be considered the indirect referral of that affiliate until they click on your link again.

Please be aware that the credits earned can be utilized on our platform in the same manner as regularly purchased credits. Currently, these credits cannot be exchanged for or withdrawn as real money. It's important to note that self-referral is strictly prohibited. If a user does not visit our platform through your link, we are unable to manually adjust their referral status due to verification limitations. The referral system operates based on browser cookies. Consequently, if the referred user registers or makes a purchase using a different browser than the one they used to click the affiliate link (for indirect referrals), they may not be recognized as your referral due to technological constraints. We reserve the right to modify the affiliate and commission rules at any time for any reason. In such instances, the most recent rules will be applicable to all users.
